项目背景

With the increasing frequency of natural disasters such as earthquakes, they have brought enormous harm to people's lives and safety. Our goal is to develop snake-like robots that can go into landslides where rescuers can't, use acoustic detectors to detect what's going on underneath landslides, and use infrared detection to detect trapped people.

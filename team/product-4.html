可交付性

The snake detection rescue robot is a technological innovation designed to enhance rescue efforts in earthquake-stricken areas. These robots are expected to deliver some important results that could significantly improve the efficiency and effectiveness of rescue operations. In addition, the robots are expected to increase the speed of rescue efforts. By quickly scanning and measuring disaster areas, they can identify blocked roads, collapsed buildings, and other obstacles that could hinder the progress of rescue teams. This information allows rescuers to determine the safest and most efficient routes to reach those trapped, speeding up the rescue process and potentially saving more lives. In addition to snake detection and wayfinding capabilities, these robots can also provide essential supplies to affected people. By carrying and distributing essential items such as food, water, medical kits and communications equipment, robots alleviate the immediate needs of those trapped in rubble or isolated due to damaged infrastructure. This vital assistance not only ensures the survival and well-being of survivors, but also allows rescue teams to focus on other critical tasks, prioritizing medical and evacuation processes. In summary, the expected deliverables of snake detection rescue robots include effective wayfinding to facilitate faster rescue operations, as well as the distribution of necessary supplies to stranded people. These results are critical to ensuring the effectiveness and success of relief efforts in quake-affected areas. By meeting these expectations, snake-detecting rescue robots can greatly improve the accuracy, speed, and overall outcome of rescue missions, ultimately reducing the impact of disasters on human lives.
